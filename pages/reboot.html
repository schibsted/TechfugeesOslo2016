Download the Reboot App to stay up to date on the agenda, give feedback to speakers and to connect with all the other fantastic attendees.

Get the iOS App

  1. Download the iOS App
  2. Run the app.
    You will get a warning saying the distributor is not trusted
  3. Go to Settings -> General -> (Profiles &) Device Management -> Schibsted Iberica S.L
  4. Click "Trust Schibsted Iberica S.L"
  5. Run the app

Get the Android App

  1. Download the Android App
  2. Run the app
